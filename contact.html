About me

I discovered a passion for creating things at a young age, and during a darkroom photography class in high school, I realised that photography was my true calling. In university, I explored photography as a means of self-expression and found beauty in capturing real people and their stories. By approaching individuals with care, I authentically portrayed their true selves, forming connections and gaining insights into my own identity. Life itself is a work of art, filled with meaningful moments. As a photographer, I strive to capture these moments with respect and authenticity. If you value genuine connections, let's create something amazing together!
