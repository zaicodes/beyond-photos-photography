Frequently Asked Questions

We are going to have fun with it! Let's work together to be creative and bring your personality to life, whether it be as an individual, pair, or group.

For individuals - I will make sure you feel as comfortable and confident as possible. Want some music? Let's turn it up. Want to try several outfits? Let's mix it up. I will make sure to be your personal guide and hype woman!

For pairs & groups - I'll give some space to connect and explore. Of course we will get a couple posed images, but most of our session will be organic and laid back. The goal is to capture authentic connection. I will make sure to provide guidance throughout the session!

Whatever makes you feel confident and comfortable!

I used to send a style guide to all clients, but I quickly realised that this isn't a one size fits all kind of situation. Let's talk about your personal style, the location, and vibe of the session and we can figure out the perfect outfit for you together. I'll make sure to point out all of the does and don'ts when choosing colours, fabrics and layers.

My current turnaround time is two weeks, but sometimes that may be shorter or longer. If you need a gallery by a certain time, please let me know when booking your session so I can make sure I am able to meet your needs.

Editing is one of my favourite parts of the process, and I think it is equally important as capturing the images. I take my time to refine each image in order to deliver an uniform, beautiful gallery to each client.

Family sessions include immediate family. Any additional family members added to the session will add an additional fee. Please contact me before booking an extended family session.

I have a lifestyle approach to newborn sessions, rather than a gallery full of posed and wrapped images. I believe that newborn sessions should represent the emotion, bond and imtimacy involving welcoming a new baby into your family!

If you would like a couple of wrapped and posed shots, please let me know before booking your session so I can make sure to fit your needs.

I think in-home sessions are the best approach for a newborn sessions. This way you can feel comfortable in your home while you take breaks for feeding, changing, and calming down your little one. Babies are unpredictable and need to be treated with patients, so please do not feel stressed or rushed during your session. I am prepared for as many breaks as we need to make sure I deliver a beautiful gallery to remember this special time in your life!

I LOVE travelling to new locations for sessions!

I recently moved from Katy to Bryan/College Station. I will travel at no cost to Katy and surrounding areas for previous clients. There will be a travel fee for any new clients that want a session 20 miles outside Bryan/College Station area. The fee depends on distance, so please contact me when booking your session.

Studio sessions can be a great options for clients who want a specific aesthetic or location.

Studio fees typically range from £75-£200, and will be added to the base price of the session.

I spent many years developing my shooting and editing style, but it is always evolving. I factor in the clothing style and colours, location, light and overall mood of the session while I edit, so some galleriies may vary slightly from each other. Typically I have a warm, earthy, moody style, but I encourage you to take a look at my gallery and instagram to get a better idea of what to expect!

If you want something specific, please send over a message so we can discuss if we will be a good fit for each other!

I do not provide RAW images to any client. I will only deliver a final product.
